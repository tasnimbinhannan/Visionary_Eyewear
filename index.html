About Visionary

Visionary is a modern eyewear concept store built entirely in the browser. It’s a demo storefront that shows how a fast, minimal e-commerce experience can look and feel.

What this site is

A polished front-end prototype built with pure HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript. There’s no real payment processing or backend — all data lives in your browser’s storage so you can safely explore flows like wishlist, cart, checkout, and order history.

How you could use it

Swap in your own products, plug into a real API, and you have a ready-to-go starting point for a real eyewear storefront — or any other product line that needs a clean, story-driven UI.

Frequently asked questions

Do you offer prescription and blue-light lenses?

In a real store you could offer both. In this demo, products are sample data only, but the UI is designed so you can label frames for blue-light use, prescription, or kids.

How long does shipping usually take?

The checkout shows “Standard” and “Express” options as an example. In production, you could connect those to real shipping rates and carriers.

What is your return or exchange policy?

This site doesn’t process real orders, but the copy and layout are ready for your own return policy — for example, “30-day hassle-free returns on all frames.”

Do you ship internationally?

You can adapt the checkout form to support multiple countries, currencies, and localized tax logic once you connect a backend.

Are my payment details stored anywhere?

No. This is a purely front-end demo. Card fields are simulation-only and are never sent or stored anywhere.
